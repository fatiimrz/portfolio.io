Chai Aur Rai

In September 2024, a South Asian coffee shop in New Jersey reached out to me through my Etsy business account, interested in wholesaling tote bags and commissioning a custom digital design for their café. Through multiple meetings, we discussed their vision—incorporating their shop colors, South Asian culture, and the phrase "Chai Aur Rai" (Let there always be tea) into a design that truly represented their café. They wanted something warm and inviting, reflecting both their cultural roots and a welcoming space for study and community. To bring their vision to life, I designed an illustration of a steaming cup of tea resting on a stack of books, each adorned with intricate Desi-inspired prints. I carefully incorporated their shop colors into the patterns and details, blending them seamlessly with traditional South Asian motifs to create a design that felt both personal and culturally authentic. The books symbolize comfort, knowledge, and storytelling, reinforcing the café’s identity as a space where everyone is welcome to sip tea, study, and connect. After rounds of sketching and refining, we finalized a design that perfectly captured their brand’s essence and cultural heritage.
