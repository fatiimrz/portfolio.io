Al Ajal Crewneck

A mosque youth group reached out to me with the opportunity to design a crewneck for them. In multiple meetings, they expressed their desire to create something that visibly showed their support for Palestine. I began sketching different concepts, incorporating the map of Palestine, but none truly captured what they envisioned. After much thought, I decided to integrate Palestinian traditions and architecture within the map itself, symbolizing the heart of Palestine—its rich culture, resilience, and history. To further reinforce the message, I included the quote “Never to humiliation”, a powerful statement that embodies strength, dignity, and unwavering support in the face of adversity. When I presented the final design, my clients absolutely loved it, expressing that it perfectly conveyed their message. They brought the design to life as a crewneck, which was then sold in the mosque, allowing members of the community to proudly wear their support for Palestine.
