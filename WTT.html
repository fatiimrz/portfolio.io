Whats The Tea

In December 2024, a South Asian coffee house in New Jersey called What's the Tea reached out to me through my Instagram business account. They were interested in wholesaling some of my South Asian-inspired tote bags and also wanted a custom tote design for their café. For the custom design, we scheduled a Zoom meeting where the owner shared her vision. She wanted two designs—one featuring their newest bubble tea drinks and another showcasing chai (Pakistani tea)—but with a subtle South Asian cultural touch while keeping the designs minimal. She specifically requested that no faces or characters be included. To bring this idea to life, I created two unique illustrations. Each design featured a hand holding the drink, but to incorporate South Asian culture, I added traditional jewelry and henna, symbolizing inclusivity and cultural heritage. I carefully replicated their signature drinks, ensuring that the details, from the cup designs to the colors, matched their brand. The final result blended their branding with South Asian elements, creating tote bags that felt personal to their café while staying true to their cultural inspiration
